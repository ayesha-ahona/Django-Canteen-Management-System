About UAP CanteenX

Welcome to UAP CanteenX 🍴 — the official canteen of University of Asia Pacific. Here, we bring you a modern food ordering experience where students, faculty, staff, and visitors can easily explore menus, place orders, and enjoy delicious meals.

Why Choose UAP CanteenX?

  • 🍔 Fresh and hygienic food prepared daily
  • ⚡ Fast service with online ordering system
  • 📊 Role-based access: Students, Faculty, Vendors, Staff all get their own dashboards
  • 💳 Multiple payment options and billing system
  • 🤖 AI-based food recommender (coming soon!)

Our goal is to make food ordering hassle-free, transparent, and accessible to everyone on campus. Whether you are a student rushing to classes, a teacher looking for quick snacks, or a guest visiting our university — UAP CanteenX is here to serve you.
